
1. Xpath with Operators
	1. and: 
	//tagname[@<attribute1>='<attribute value>' and @<attribute2>='<attribute value>']
	
	2. or:
	//tagname[@<attribute1>='<attribute value>' or @<attribute2>='<attribute value>']

2. Xpath With indexing
	2.1 With Index:
		(//tagname[@<attribute1>='<attribute value>'])[index]

	2.2	With position(index): 
		(//tagname[@<attribute1>='<attribute value>'])[position()=index]
	
3. Xpath Methods
	3.1	text(): This method never ignore white space and it fails if text has white space
		//tagname[text()='visible text']
	
	3.2	normalize-space(): this method ignore white space (It act like trim()from String)
		//tagname[normalize-space()='visible text']
	
	3.3	contains():
		//tagname[contains(@<attribute>,'<substring>')]
		or
		//tagname[contains(text(),'<substring>')]
		or
		//tagname[contains(normalize-space(),'<substring>')]

	3.4	starts-with()
		//tagname[starts-with(@<attribute>,'<Prefix string>')]
		Or	
		//tagname[starts-with(text(),'<Prefix string>')]
		Or
		//tagname[starts-with(normalize-space(),'<Prefix string>')]

4. Xpath Axis

	4.1	ancestor:
		//tagname[@<attribute>='<attribute value>']//ancestor::tagname of ancestor
			
	4.2	parent:
		//tagname[@<attribute>='<attribute value>']//parent::tagname of parent
			
	4.3	child:
		//tagname[@<attribute>='<attribute value>']//child::tagname of child
			
	4.4	following:
		//tagname[@<attribute>='<attribute value>']//following::tagname of following
	
	4.5	following-sibling:
		//tagname[@<attribute>='<attribute value>']//following-sibling::tagname of following-sibling
		
	4.6	preceding:
		//tagname[@<attribute>='<attribute value>']//preceding::tagname of preceding element
		
	4.7	preceding-sibling:
		//tagname[@<attribute>='<attribute value>']//preceding-sibling::tagname of preceding-sibling
